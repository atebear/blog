I didn’t plan to tell anyone about this blog. Honestly.

I thought I should maybe at least talk about it to Mama Bear, but I’m too shy 🤭

But 4 days ago, I randomly and excitedly told one of my sisters in Christ — my (un)official disciple — about it 😅

The moment I told her, I immediately regretted it 🤦‍♀️\
I didn’t know how she would react to it.

But thankfully, her response was very positive. She was really supportive about it.

I told her some of my plans, the things I want to write about, and more.

Today, I mentioned to her that [I posted an update yesterday](/notes/still-a-bear-family). I honestly don’t know what I was thinking, but I even let her read it while waiting for her comments.

She didn’t react that much at first, but I realized maybe it didn’t really matter. I was just being myself, you know?

Then moments later, she told me she wanted to create an account and start blogging too.

*(I guess I did pretty well? 😆 JK)*

I wasn’t surprised, but I was really happy for her. She’s the kind of person who likes trying everything.

So I assisted her through the site, especially with Markdown since it’s new to her. I think this is also her first blogging platform.

It’s crazy how meaningful it feels to share something you genuinely love with someone who truly appreciates it.

Seeing her earlier reminded me of my younger self. That same excitement. That same fun. And that same enthusiasm.

You can visit her blog at → [**Foxy The Fox!**](https://foxy.bearblog.dev) εᐟᐠ>ﻌ<ᐟᐠз

I gave her the nickname **“Foxy”** because she likes foxes. She said she’s <mark style="background-color: #FFFF00">very sneaky, chaotic, and fast</mark> like one 🦊

She actually wrote about [how she discovered this blogging platform through me](https://foxy.bearblog.dev/how-did-i-find-this-mysterious-website) and [how she came up with the name of her blog](https://foxy.bearblog.dev/why). She even linked my blog, so to return the favor, I’m linking hers too 😂

My message to her:

> If you’re reading this, Foxy 🦊\
> I’m lowkey looking forward to what you’ll write in your future posts.
>
> Just stay true to yourself.\
> Share your thoughts. Express yourself.
>
> Use this platform not only to share your story, but also to share your faith.
>
> God bless you, Foxy 🤍 —Ate Bear ʕ•ᴥ•ʔ
