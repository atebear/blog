I’m still very sad about it.

My Bear family is no longer attending our church.

It’s been 2 weeks and 3 days since Papa Bear told our pastor that they would no longer be attending church with us. I won’t disclose or mention the reason here. Thank you for understanding.

With that, Mama Bear, Gummy Bear, and Little Bear also left with Papa Bear.

I wasn’t there when everything happened. On May 10, I didn’t attend church because I had a stomachache.

I heard the news two days later. At first, it didn’t fully sink in until the following day. I felt very sad, so I messaged Mama Bear through Messenger.

She told me that she was also deeply saddened, but she uplifted my spirit by saying, “I know God will turn it for good.”

On my first Sunday without them, church didn’t feel the same. It felt like something was missing. But God comforted my soul.

Now, I can only see them during special occasions, but I know nothing will change between us.

We’re still a Bear family. ʕ•ᴥ•ʔ
