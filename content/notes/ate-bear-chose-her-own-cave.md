Hey Bear 👋

This post might be a little disheartening to some, but I've genuinely enjoyed every moment I've spent here on [BearBlog](https://bearblog.dev).

After a lot of thought and consideration, I've decided to make [atebear.pages.dev](https://atebear.pages.dev) the primary home of **ʕ•ᴥ•ʔ Ate Bear Blog**.

I want to own my content and have full control over my little corner of the web.

My site is <mark>open source, decentralized, free, and doesn't track its visitors.</mark> It also means I only need to maintain one site instead of juggling multiple homes for the same blog.

Don't worry—Ate Bear Blog isn't going anywhere.

The future plans I have for this little cave will continue. I'm simply choosing a different place as my main territory.

BearBlog was where this journey began, and I'm grateful for that.

So this isn't really a goodbye.

It's more like a bear moving to a new cave while waving back at the old one.

Thank you for welcoming me here.

Godspeed! 🐻🏕️✨
